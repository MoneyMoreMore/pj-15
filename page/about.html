艾瑞克

ELLRICKY (Eric) was founded in 1989 in Montpellier, France (Montpelier).
It is an art educational institution focused on cultivating children's artistic creativity. It is known as another breakthrough in children's education after Hua Defu. ELLRICKY lets children discover the laws of beauty in life through a scientific and systematic art course, and lets them use their own artistic language to express beauty and create beauty. ELLRICKY refuses rigid teaching and gives children a free play. Their creativity and imagination will constantly surprise you! During the course, children's observation and cognitive ability, hands-on ability, collaborative ability, language communication ability will be strengthened, comprehensively enhance the creativity of children! Help children solve all kinds of problems in life and conquer all kinds of challenges. We sincerely hope to inspire every child's potential through the ELLRICKY curriculum. They implant the seeds of art and creation in their hearts, so that they can enjoy life from the artist's unique perspective, because life should be more beautiful. ELLRICKY came to China in 2014 to bring better art education resources to more Chinese families. Advanced concepts, international vision, wonderful courses, so that we can better show the charm of art, so that more children have the unique temperament and accomplishment of artists, designers and aesthetic wisdom.
